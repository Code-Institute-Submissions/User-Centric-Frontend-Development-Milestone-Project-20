Re-cycle Club Tours

Athletic Tours

We have a team of members who take part in different races around the world including "Tour de France". We facilitate training with a skilled team member specific to your needs. Weather you want to try busy cycling or long race cycling, we got you! We have regular weekly days trips around spontaneous route which you will enjoy. Just bring your gear and if you forgot it in another place we have different models of cycles to suit your needs. Why not join them to motivate yourself around people which the same goals.

Family Tours

We bring together families members of all ages on an adventure trip around the country side with interesting activities and meals during the breaks. Bring your picnic bucket or order on the details so that our team can arrange that for you. Meet other families on organized family fun tours and enjoy a lovely day. Tell us your preferences so that we can take your wishes into consideration.

“The experience of riding on our eco-friendly routes, with our eco-friendly modified cycles has been an exciting adventure for our users. ”

City Tours

Tourists are welcome to use our eco-friendly bicycles delivered at the airport. We can handle your luggage for you while you cycle around. Our insurance cover protects your belongings while you cycle around. If you are a backpacker we have storage for your belonging while you cycle away. Are you on a shopping spree? Then a bucket provided to carry all you shopping. Avoid the weights.

Leisure Tours

Do you plan to have a social time with your friends in a way that safe the environment from climate change disasters. Guess what? We got you. Our eco-friendly bicycles are an answer to those worries. Leave your cars home and cycle to the mountains or a nearby Storoya beach in Fornebu is one of the places not to be missed. Swap using buses to an experience of cycling around interesting parks places and restaurant hopping with a bicycle.

Adventure Tours

Our clients have different passions including adventures sports and we cover that. Some of our team members are living the life of adventure with bicycles and use the services we provide to access more adventure sport venues. Join them and learn for others or explore with them. With us you are never alone. Its a passion and we support it. Or is it art? Then you on!

Discovery Tours

A team of members have interest in exploring interesting historic sites of Norway through cycling. Join them and Discovery the beauty of Norway on a bicycle. We will organize it all and insurance is included. We cover long trips outside Asker and to other tourist destinations around Norway. You not alone in this, a team is already doing the trips. Just join them and have fun!
